What are the manali package tour destinations?

 

Manali is a popular tourist destination located in the northern Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. The town is known for its snow-capped peaks, lush green forests, gushing rivers, and ancient temples. It is a perfect destination for travelers who want to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and spend some time in a serene environment. Manali package tour destinations offer a comprehensive tour of this picturesque town and cater to the needs of travelers of all kinds. In this article, we will discuss some of the best Manali package tour destinations that one can explore during their visit to this mesmerizing town.

 

Rohtang Pass is one of the most popular Manali package tour destinations. It is a high mountain pass located at an altitude of 13,050 feet above sea level. The pass is famous for its scenic beauty and adventure activities like skiing, snowboarding, and trekking. It is one of the most sought-after destinations in Manali and is included in most Manali tour packages. The pass is closed during winters due to heavy snowfall, but it is open from May to November.

 

Solang Valley is another popular Manali package tour destination that offers breathtaking views of the Himalayas. The valley is located between Solang village and Beas Kund and is known for adventure activities like paragliding, skiing, and zorbing. The valley is surrounded by snow-capped peaks and offers a serene environment for visitors to relax and rejuvenate. Solang Valley is included in most Manali tour packages and is a must-visit for adventure enthusiasts.

 

Hadimba Temple is an ancient temple located in the heart of Manali. It is dedicated to Goddess Hadimba, who is believed to be the wife of Bhima from the Mahabharata. The temple is located amidst a dense forest of deodar trees and offers a serene environment for visitors to relax and rejuvenate. The temple is included in most Manali tour packages and is a must-visit for anyone visiting Manali.

 

Vashisht Hot Springs is another popular Manali package tour destination that is known for its hot springs and ancient temples. The hot springs are believed to have medicinal properties and are free to visit. The ancient temple dedicated to Sage Vashisht is also worth visiting. The temple is believed to be more than 4000 years old and is a perfect destination for those interested in ancient history and culture.

 

Apart from these popular Manali package tour destinations, there are many other places that one can explore during their visit to Manali. Beas Kund is a serene lake located amidst snow-capped peaks and is a popular destination for trekking. Manikaran is a famous pilgrimage site that is known for its hot springs and ancient temples. The town is situated on the banks of the Parvati river and is a popular destination for Hindu and Sikh pilgrims. These destinations are also included in most Manali tour packages and are a must-visit for anyone interested in exploring the rich cultural and natural heritage of the region.
